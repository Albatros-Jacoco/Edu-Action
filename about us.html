Concept

In today's world, Inovative Educating Systems are being on eveyones' mouth. However, although most systems might be structural different, they are stil saving old meaning which has not more value in today's developing world rather than it had in the past. Now teachers have to teach individually and uniquly every student. As well as, teachers have to be teached in everyday due to the secondly growth of information. They need assistant to get some responsiblities on teachers' shoulders and cover more and more students on modern educating. We believe Technologies can never replace on with teachers and on working the project, I have kept in my mind Bill Gates' words, "Technology is just a tool. In terms of getting the kids working together and motivating them. The teacher is the most important."
